Naive Bayes Classifier

It is a classifier algorithm  that applies Bayes theorem.

Naive Bayes is a conditional probablity model  in which P(A/B) = (P(B/A)*P(A))/P(B). Bayes theorem describe the relationship between P(A/B) , P(B) , P(A) and P(B/A)
 here P(A) is the probablity that A is true and also known as Prior probablity
P(A/B) is the posterior probablity of A in B observation
